Our Story

Community Connect was founded in 2010 with a simple goal: to bring people together to solve local problems. What started as a small neighborhood initiative has grown into a city-wide movement.

Over the years, we've connected thousands of volunteers with hundreds of community projects, from park cleanups to after-school programs for at-risk youth.
